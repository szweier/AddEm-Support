Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 18, 2024

Overview

Add 'em ("the App") is a daily number puzzle game. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. All game data, including your statistics, achievements, and progress, is stored locally on your device.

Local Data Storage

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Game statistics (puzzles completed, solve times, streaks)
  • Achievement progress
  • App preferences and settings

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Notifications

The App may request permission to send local notifications to remind you of daily puzzles. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.

Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
