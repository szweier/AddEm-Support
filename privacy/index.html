Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Add 'Em ("we", "our", or "the app") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Add 'Em is designed with your privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not require an account or login
  • Does not collect your name, email, or any personal identifiers
  • Does not track your location
  • Does not use analytics or tracking services
  • Does not contain advertisements

Data Stored on Your Device

Add 'Em stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your puzzle completion history and statistics
  • Your current streak information
  • App preferences and settings
  • Achievement progress

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any servers.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are scheduled locally on your device. We do not use push notification services that require transmitting data to external servers.

Sharing Features

When you use the share feature to share your results or challenge friends, the app generates a shareable image and text. This sharing is handled entirely by your device's native sharing functionality, and we do not have access to or store any information about what you share or with whom.

Children's Privacy

Add 'Em does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Third-Party Services

Add 'Em does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted, your information is protected by your device's built-in security features.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You can clear all app data by deleting the app from your device.
