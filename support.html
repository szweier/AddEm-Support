Welcome to Add 'Em support! Find answers to common questions below or contact us directly.

How to Play

Add 'Em is a daily number puzzle game. Your goal is to clear the board by selecting groups of adjacent hexagons that sum to the target number.

  • Drag across hexagons to select a group
  • Selected hexagons must be adjacent (touching)
  • When the sum equals the target, the hexagons disappear
  • Clear all hexagons to win!

Frequently Asked Questions

When does a new puzzle appear?

A new puzzle is available every day at midnight Eastern Time (ET). Each day's puzzle is the same for all players worldwide.

Can I play previous puzzles?

Yes! Tap the calendar icon to access the Archive and play any puzzle from the past.

What is a streak?

Your streak counts consecutive days you've completed the daily puzzle. Keep playing every day to build your streak!

How do hints work?

Tap the lightbulb icon to highlight a valid group you can select. Hints show you one possible move but won't solve the puzzle for you.

What do the difficulty badges mean?

Each puzzle has a difficulty rating: Easy (green), Medium (yellow), or Hard (red). This is based on the puzzle's complexity.

How do challenges work?

After completing a puzzle, tap "Challenge a Friend" to share your time. When your friend opens the link, they'll play the same puzzle and compare times!

Is my data backed up?

Your stats and progress are stored locally on your device. If you delete the app, this data will be lost. We recommend keeping the app installed to preserve your streak and achievements.

Why can't I find a solution?

Every puzzle is guaranteed to be solvable! If you're stuck, try using a hint or the undo button to try a different approach. Sometimes the solution requires selecting groups in a specific order.
